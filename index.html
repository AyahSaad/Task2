Ramallah Al-Mazra'a ash-Sharqiya

Al-Mazra'a ash-Sharqiya is a center located in the State of Palestine, specifically within the administrative region of Ramallah and Al-Bireh Governorate.
Geographically, it sits at an elevation of 1 kilometer above sea level. According to a census conducted in 2017, the population of Al-Mazra'a ash-Sharqiya reached 4,063 residents.
