Crepello Palestine

Criblo Restaurant, located in Ramallah Al-Masyoun next to the Caesar Hotel and across from Nelson Mandela Square, offers a wide array of delicious dishes, including pizza, various sandwiches, and pasta in different shapes and sauces, along with fresh salads. It specializes particularly in exquisite Western desserts, such as a variety of sweet pastries. With its comfortable seating and a modern yet simple design, Criblo is a must-visit if you're in or near Ramallah.
